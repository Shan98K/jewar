Nestled on a serene hilltop in the heart of Burgundy, Château du Feÿ is more than just a wedding venue — it’s a place where sustainability meets timeless beauty, offering a warm, luxurious setting for your special day. This 17th-century château, a designated Historical Monument of France, has been lovingly preserved to maintain its historical charm while embracing modern principles of design, conviviality, and eco-consciousness.


Château du Feÿ offers a variety of unique settings that combine the rustic charm of the French countryside with luxurious touches, allowing you to create a wedding that is as stylish as it is authentic and welcoming:

The Gardens : Host your ceremony or reception in one of the many breathtaking spots within the estate’s 40 hectares of gardens. Imagine exchanging vows under the 400-year-old oak trees

The Terrace : A chic space ideal for a cocktail hour, where your guests can enjoy panoramic views of the valley.

The Main Chateau : For more intimate receptions, the historic interiors of Chateau du Feÿ provide a cozy and elegant ambiance.

The Press Area and Barns : These spaces offer a unique blend of rustic charm and refined elegance, perfect for larger gatherings.

The Orangerie : This 450-square-meter designer tent, nestled within the château’s vegetable garden, offers a spacious setting for all-night celebrations.

Accommodation : Chateau du Feÿ can accommodate up to 160 guests on site—70 in the château and its farmhouses, and 90 in glamping tents facing a beautiful, open field.
